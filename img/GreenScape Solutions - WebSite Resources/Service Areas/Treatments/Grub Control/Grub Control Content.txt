What is Grub Control?

Grub control is the practice of managing the population of "white grubs"—the larval stage of various beetles (such as Japanese Beetles, June Bugs, and European Chafers)—to prevent them from destroying your lawn.

Grubs live underground and feed exclusively on the roots of your grass. Because they eat the "anchor" and the nutrient delivery system of the plant, a heavy infestation can kill large sections of a lawn in a very short amount of time.

==============================

==============================

Why is Grub Control Important?

Grub control is often considered the "insurance policy" of a professional lawn care program. Because grubs live underground and are invisible until the damage is done, a sudden infestation can be one of the most disheartening and expensive problems a homeowner can face.
