About Us

Founded in 2005, Green Star LLC in Delaware, Ohio, is dedicated to providing top-notch fertilizer and lawn care services, ensuring that your lawn remains healthy and vibrant throughout the year. With 18 years of experience, our team is equipped to handle various lawn conditions, from clay soils and high-traffic areas to poor water retention and nutrient competition. Our comprehensive lawn care program includes seasonal treatments that promote a lush, green, and weed-free lawn. Additionally, we offer aeration services to oxygenate the soil, strengthen roots, and encourage new growth in patchy areas. Our innovative fertilizer carbon treatments further enhance soil health, fostering a robust lawn.

At Green Star LLC, customer satisfaction is our highest priority. We pride ourselves on being open and honest with our customers, ensuring thorough follow-ups and a job well done. Our commitment to exceptional customer service sets us apart from the competition. Discover the difference our expertise and dedication can make for your lawn.
